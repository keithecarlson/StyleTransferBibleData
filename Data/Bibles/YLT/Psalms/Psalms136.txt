1 Give ye thanks to Jehovah, For good, for to the age  His kindness.
2 Give ye thanks to the God of gods, For to the age  His kindness.
3 Give ye thanks to the Lord of lords, For to the age  His kindness.
4 To Him doing great wonders by Himself alone, For to the age  His kindness.
5 To Him making the heavens by understanding, For to the age  His kindness.
6 To Him spreading the earth over the waters, For to the age  His kindness.
7 To Him making great lights, For to the age  His kindness.
8 The sun to rule by day, For to the age  His kindness.
9 The moon and stars to rule by night, For to the age  His kindness.
10 To Him smiting Egypt in their first-born, For to the age  His kindness.
11 And bringing forth Israel from their midst, For to the age  His kindness.
12 By a strong hand, and a stretched-out-arm, For to the age  His kindness.
13 To Him cutting the sea of Suph into parts, For to the age  His kindness,
14 And caused Israel to pass through its midst, For to the age  His kindness,
15 And shook out Pharaoh and his force in the sea of Suph, For to the age  His kindness.
16 To Him leading His people in a wilderness, For to the age  His kindness.
17 To Him smiting great kings, For to the age  His kindness.
18 Yea, He doth slay honourable kings, For to the age  His kindness.
19 Even Sihon king of the Amorite, For to the age  His kindness.
20 And Og king of Bashan, For to the age  His kindness.
21 And He gave their land for inheritance, For to the age  His kindness.
22 An inheritance to Israel His servant, For to the age  His kindness.
23 Who in our lowliness hath remembered us, For to the age  His kindness.
24 And He delivereth us from our adversaries, For to the age  His kindness.
25 Giving food to all flesh, For to the age  His kindness.
26 Give ye thanks to the God of the heavens, For to the age  His kindness!
